Lasagna

Description

A delicious 3-cheese lasagna for the whole family. This recipe easily feeds 4-6 people and is one of my grandma's favorites. Feel free to substitute the ricotta with cream cheese to mix things up!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef (97/3)
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup freshly chopped basil
  • 1 14oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon groudn oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot, saut√© the ground beef. Add the garlic and onion and continue cooking until the meat is browned and the onions have softened. Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 1 hour.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles in a large pot and drain them with a colander before they reach al dente.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the parsley and salt. Place the bowl in the fridge until it's time to assemble the lasagna.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  5. In a deep 9x13 pan, begin assembling the lasagna by placing a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Cover this layer with noodles before topping with the ricotta cheese followed by a layer of sliced mozzarella. Cover this layer with meat sauce then top with the Parmesan cheese. Repeat this process 2 more times.
  6. Cover the pan with aluminum foil and bake for 15 minutes.
  7. Optional: broil with the foil removed for an additional 2 minutes to brown the cheese before serving.
