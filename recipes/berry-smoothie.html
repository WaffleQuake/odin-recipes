Berry Smoothie

Description

The perfect smoothie recipe for a hot summer day. It's so good, you might decide you would rather drink this over your favorite hot cocoa on a cold winter day. This recipe is for 1 serving, making it easy to measure for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 medium apple, sliced
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the milk, yogurt, mixed berries, apple slices, and honey. Blend until smooth (be sure to cover the blender). Pour into a tall glass to serve.
