Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe will knock your socks off! This version is for a soft, chewy cookie, but you can easily bake them a little longer for a crispier feel. This recipe yields 4 dozen cookies, so you will have plenty of dough to freeze and use later.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softned
  • 2 cups chopped pecans (optional)
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semi-sweet choicolate chips
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the butter and sugar with an electric mixer for several minutes until smooth.
  2. Add the eggs and the vanilla and continue mixing.
  3. Dissolve baking soda into 2 tablespoons of hot water and add into the bowl.
  4. Add flour, chocolate chips, and pecans.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  6. Chill the dough for 5 minutes in the fridge.
  7. Place balls of dough 2 inches apart on a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool completely before serving.
